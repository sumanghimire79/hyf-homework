momo recepie

ingredients

* chicken 1 kg
* cabbage 2 kg
* wheat floor 1 kg

procedure

1. chopp the chicken in to small pices
2. chopp the cabbage in to small pices
3. mix chicken and cabbage
4. add salt , chilli, spices as per taste
5. make the dough 
6. make small circle pastry from the douch
7. take spoon and put the mixture inside the pastry and pack it gently
8. cook the momos for around 25-30 minutes in water steam
9. serve hot momo with tomato pickle
